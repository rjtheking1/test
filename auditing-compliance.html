What is Auditing & Compliance?

Our Auditing & Compliance Guidance service helps small and medium-sized businesses navigate the complex world of regulatory compliance and security audits.

We provide practical tools, templates, and guidance to help you prepare for audits, maintain compliance, and demonstrate your commitment to data security and privacy.

Target Audience

Small offices aiming for regulatory readiness and businesses seeking compliance certifications

Tools We Provide

  • Excel / Google Sheets Templates

    Ready-to-use spreadsheets for compliance reporting and tracking

  • Audit Checklists

    Comprehensive checklists for various compliance frameworks

  • Compliance Guides

    Step-by-step guides for meeting regulatory requirements

  • Reporting Dashboards

    Visual dashboards to track compliance metrics

Compliance Frameworks We Cover

GDPR

General Data Protection Regulation - EU data privacy and protection compliance for businesses handling European customer data.

PCI-DSS

Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard - Essential for businesses processing credit card payments securely.

ISO 27001

International standard for information security management systems - demonstrates your commitment to security.

SOC 2

Service Organization Control 2 - Trust principles for service providers handling customer data securely.

HIPAA

Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act - For healthcare providers and businesses handling medical data.

IT Act (India)

Information Technology Act - Indian cyber law compliance for digital businesses and data protection.
