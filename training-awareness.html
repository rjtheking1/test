Security Training / Awareness

Short, engaging workshops to build a security-aware culture in your office

Why Security Awareness?

Your employees are your first line of defense against cyber threats. Our Security Training & Awareness programs equip your team with the knowledge and skills to identify and prevent security incidents.

We deliver practical, engaging workshops that transform security from a technical concept into everyday practice for your entire organization.

Target Audience

Office staff at all levels - from executives to entry-level employees who need practical security knowledge

Training Tools

  • PowerPoint / Google Slides

    Professional presentation materials for engaging workshops

  • Demo Labs (Kali Linux)

    Hands-on demonstrations of real attack scenarios

  • Phishing Simulations

    Practice attacks to test and improve awareness

  • Video Tutorials

    Short, digestible videos for on-demand learning

Workshop Topics

Phishing Awareness

Learn to identify phishing emails, suspicious links, and social engineering tactics used by attackers.

Password Security

Best practices for creating strong passwords, using password managers, and enabling multi-factor authentication.

Mobile Device Security

Securing smartphones and tablets used for work, including app permissions and public Wi-Fi safety.

Data Protection

Handling sensitive data responsibly, encryption basics, and compliance with privacy regulations.

Remote Work Security

Securing home networks, VPN usage, and maintaining security while working from anywhere.

Incident Response

What to do when you suspect a security breach - reporting procedures and immediate actions.
